Essential Care Guidelines

Master the fundamentals of plant care specific to Islamabad's climate

Watering Strategy

  • Water early morning or late evening to reduce evaporation
  • Deep watering 2-3 times weekly beats daily shallow watering
  • Adjust frequency based on monsoon rainfall in summer
  • Use drip irrigation for water conservation

Soil Preparation

  • Add organic compost to improve clay-heavy Islamabad soil
  • Maintain pH between 6.0-7.0 for most vegetables
  • Mix sand for better drainage in monsoon season
  • Mulch heavily to retain moisture in summer

Sun Protection

  • Use shade cloth (30-50%) during peak summer heat
  • Position sensitive plants for morning sun exposure
  • Create natural shade with taller companion plants
  • Move containers to shaded areas in extreme heat

Pest Management

  • Inspect plants weekly for early pest detection
  • Use neem oil spray for organic pest control
  • Companion plant with marigolds and basil
  • Remove infected leaves immediately to prevent spread

Fertilization Schedule

  • Apply balanced NPK fertilizer monthly during growing season
  • Use organic compost tea for weekly feeding
  • Add calcium for tomatoes to prevent blossom end rot
  • Reduce feeding in winter dormant periods

Winter Protection

  • Cover sensitive plants during frost warnings
  • Move potted plants indoors on coldest nights
  • Apply thick mulch layer for root protection
  • Prune frost-damaged parts only after last frost
